Bawarchi my favourite restaurant.

The nearest tourist spot to restaurant is Charminar. Also there are good shopping malls for all the age groups and adventurous places nearby the restaurant to explore

Directions to airport from restaurant

The closest airport from food court is Rajiv Gandhi International Airport.

    Below are the directions for travelling to foodcourt from airport
  • once you entered airport, you can find out the terminal to come out
    • Come out from international terminal and get into airport shuttle to Uppal cross roads which is available every one hour.
    • From Uppal cross roads, take metro to Jubilee hills No.5 cross roads which is available every 10 minutes.
    • From Jubilee hills No.5, take right and go straight till Wipro junction.
    • From Wipro junction, take left and you can see Bawarchi restaurant on the right next to Forum mall.
